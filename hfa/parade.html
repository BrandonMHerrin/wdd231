Subic Bay Ocean Adventure

This summer while visiting family in the Philippines. We decided to enroll our kids in pre-school while we were here for seven weeks. This provided a good oportunity for the kids to see what school is like for kids in the Philippines and see what school would have been like for their mother.

Our kids were invited to participate in the pre-school prince and princess parade where all the kids from the preschools in province were invited to participate in a parade and competion.

It was a fun experience for our twins especially for my daughter. who loved wearing the beatiful dress. The kids walked a parade route and got to wave to all the on lookers.

It was a got day and we all were sweating plenty, but we made some unforgetabble memories. Life is great.

Morong Prince and Princess Parade

The Morong Prince and Princess Parade allows pre-school aged kids to participate in a parade in the province where kids from the different Barangays represent their school and Barangays and are judged in a format similar to a beauty pageant. Their are prizes for the top contestants.
