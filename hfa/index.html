Who Are We?

We are fun loving chaotic family. Every moment is a new adventure. Personalities colide in the beautiful tapestry that is life. We are the uniting of two great cultures through love and understanding. We embrace our American and Filipino cultures full squeezing all the joy and greatness we can from this life.

What Do We Do?

We love spending time together. The moments we spend together are the most special moments in our lives.

Where Do We Go?

We love exploring this amazing blue planet we call home. We love to explore Asia, America, or Central America, whether by boat, plane, or larger than life 15 passenger van.

Why Do We Do It?

We believe that the greatest gift we have been given is the time we have together. We understand that life is fragile and that you never know how much time you have with your family so cherish every moment.
