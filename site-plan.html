Website Plan

Site Name

For this website I chose the name Herrin Family Adventures. I chose this name to be clear on the content of the website.

Site Purpose

To help family and friends keep up to date with the most recent adventures of our family. We have friends and family all over the world as I'm from the United States and my wife is from the Philippines. We love to see the world and spend time with family.

Scenarios

  • Where is Brandon and his family at?
  • What is going on in Brandon and his family's life?
  • Where was it that Brandon and his went last year?
  • What time is the time difference like between that location and where I am?
  • What is the weather like there?
  • What was it like going to the dolphin show in the Philippines?

Color Schema

  • Primary: #2A6352, which is a green color. this will be used for the header and footer backgrounds as well as for text in the body.
  • Secondary: #F4EAE0, which is a lighter color, this color is used as the main background.
  • Accent: #F76C5E, this is a coral color that will be used for buttons accenting different things.

Typography

Logos and Buttons

I will be using the Caveat font for the logo and for buttons. This is a more playful font that works well with the overall feel of the website.

Headers and Body Text

I chose to go with the Nunito font. I think it looks casual and works well.
